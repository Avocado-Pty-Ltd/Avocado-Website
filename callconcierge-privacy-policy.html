Privacy Policy

How CallConcierge collects, uses, and protects your information

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Avocado Pty Ltd (ACN 652 199 687) ("we", "us", "our") operates CallConcierge, an AI-powered business receptionist mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the CallConcierge application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

By using CallConcierge, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Please also review our Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an CallConcierge account, we collect:

  • Authentication Data: Email address and name from your Google or Microsoft account via OAuth
  • Personal Phone Number: Your phone number for call transfers and verification
  • Business Name: The name of your business for AI agent identification

2.2 Business Configuration

To set up your AI receptionist, we collect:

  • Knowledge Base: Information about your business, services, and how you want calls handled
  • Agent Settings: AI voice preferences, greeting messages, and behaviour configuration
  • Operating Hours: Your business timezone and availability settings

2.3 Communications Data

Through your use of CallConcierge, we process:

  • Call Recordings: Audio recordings of calls handled by your AI receptionist
  • Call Transcripts: Text transcriptions of call conversations
  • SMS Messages: Inbound and outbound text messages sent through your CallConcierge number
  • Call Metadata: Call duration, timestamps, caller phone numbers, and call outcomes

2.4 Contact Information

If you use the contacts feature:

  • Business Contacts: Names, phone numbers, and notes for contacts you add
  • Service Interests: Information about what services contacts are interested in

2.5 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Push Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens for notifications
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and actions taken
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and diagnostic data via Sentry

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the CallConcierge AI receptionist service
  • Provision and manage your dedicated business phone number
  • Process incoming calls and route them to the AI agent
  • Send and receive SMS messages on your behalf
  • Deliver push notifications for incoming calls and messages
  • Generate call summaries and transcripts
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Provide customer support
  • Send service-related communications
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

CallConcierge integrates with the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Firebase (Google) Authentication, database, push notifications Account data, app data, device tokens
Twilio Phone number provisioning, calls, SMS Phone numbers, call audio, messages
RetellAI AI voice agent processing Call audio, knowledge base, transcripts
RevenueCat Subscription management User ID, purchase data
Sentry Error tracking and diagnostics Error logs, device information

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Twilio Privacy Policy
  • RetellAI Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Sentry Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Location

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Account data and app data are stored on Google Cloud servers
  • Twilio: Call recordings and SMS messages are processed through Twilio's global infrastructure
  • RetellAI: AI processing occurs on RetellAI's servers

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Australia, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Account Data: While your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion
  • Call Recordings: 90 days from the date of the call
  • Call Transcripts: 1 year from the date of the call
  • SMS Messages: 1 year from the date sent/received
  • Call History: 1 year from the date of the call
  • Contacts: While your account is active
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymised indefinitely

You can request earlier deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data is transmitted using TLS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Stored data is encrypted on secure servers
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth-based login through Google and Microsoft
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations to personal data
  • Regular Security Reviews: Ongoing assessment of security practices

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below.

9. Australian Privacy Principles Compliance

We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), including:

  • APP 1: Open and transparent management of personal information
  • APP 5: Notification of collection of personal information
  • APP 6: Use and disclosure only for intended purposes
  • APP 11: Security of personal information
  • APP 12: Access to personal information on request
  • APP 13: Correction of personal information

10. Children's Privacy

CallConcierge is intended for business use by individuals aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through the CallConcierge app

Continued use of CallConcierge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
